About 

Hello, this is FengQM. You can see my curriculum vitae on this page.

This website was founded in the summer of 2021, which is similar to many personal websites. At that time, inspired by other personal websites, I built my own website.

In the first three years, the development direction was different from what I expected. It did not become a technology blog. Because in the process of constantly writing content, I gradually feel that it is not as interesting to talk about technology with others as it is to talk about life and ideas with others.

I don't like to talk about politics and society. I like different cultures and all kinds of ideas, all kinds of things and things that I haven't seen, heard or idealized. At the same time, go deep into yourself and explore free will. If you and I are willing to share ourselves

So before I write the article later, I will think about what I have here and what ideas others haven't mentioned. Whether these contents are useful after writing, whether they are interesting.At the same time, I also hope that my words can make you feel quiet and slow when reading, even if you may just pass by in a hurry.
